Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when visiting tablesgenerator.com (the "Site").

Personal information we collect

When you visit the Site, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including your web browser, IP address, time zone, and some of the cookies installed on your device. We refer to this automatically-collected information as "Device Information."

We collect Device Information using the following technologies:

  • "Cookies" are data files that are placed on your device or computer and often include a unique anonymous identifier. For more information about cookies and how to disable cookies, visit http://www.allaboutcookies.org .

  • "Log files" track actions occurring on the Site, and collect data including your IP address, browser type, referring/exit pages, and date/time stamps. Log files are stored for up to 30 days.

Voluntary correspondence

When you use the Contact form to write to us with a question or ask for help, we keep that correspondence, including the email address, to have a history of past correspondences to reference if you reach out in the future. We'll never sell this information to third parties.

Non-personal information

Part of the functionality provided by the Site requires sending data related to the table edited with the help of the Site. This data, including the table contents, is processed only to provide the requested functionality and not stored permanently.

How do we use your personal information?

  • We use the Device Information that we collect to help us screen for potential errors and improve and optimize our Site (for example, by generating analytics about how our customers browse and interact with the Site).

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our Site or other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites or other sites on the Internet.

  • Users may opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting:

    • settings provided by Google
    • YourAdChoices
    • or by using our Cookie settings provided in the footer of the Site.

  • We also use Google Analytics to help us understand how our users use the Site -- you can read more about how Google uses your personal information here: https://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy/. You can also opt-out of Google Analytics here: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout or by using our Cookie settings provided in the Site's footer.

Do not track

Please note that we do not alter our Site's data collection and use practices when seeing a Do Not Track signal from your browser.

Location of Site and data

Our Site and other web properties are operated in the United States. If you are located in the European Union or elsewhere outside of the United States, please be aware that any information you provide to us will be transferred to and stored in the United States. By using our Site, participating in any of our services, or providing us with your information, you consent to the transfer.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect, for example changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.
